Carbonara

da pasta

Ingredients

  • 100g guanciale
  • 1tsp olive oil
  • 150g spaghettoni
  • 500mL water
  • 1tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 100g pecorino romano
  • 1tsp pepper

Steps

  1. Slice the guanciale into chunks
  2. Add olive oil and the guanciale into a cold pan, heat the pan to medium low
  3. Fry guanciale until fat has been rendered and browned, remove pan from heat
  4. Boil pasta in salted water
  5. While the pasta is cooking, mix the egg, egg yolk, pecorino romano, and pepper
  6. When pasta is done, move to pan with guanciale and transfer a tablespoon of pasta water
  7. While pasta is still hot, add egg slurry and mix rigorously
